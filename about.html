About

A boutique practice, built on depth.

Blossom Keys Coaching & Training is a boutique consulting, talent-development and coaching practice, founded by professionals with deep roots in organisational consulting and talent development.

Who we are

Deep roots, practical results.

We bring consulting and leadership development together — because lasting change has to reach both the business and the people within it.

Consulting

Our consulting lead brings expertise from her tenure with PwC Consulting and the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP), where she led organisation-transformation initiatives, designed scalable operating models, and facilitated change across diverse sectors. Her strength lies in diagnosing the root causes of performance bottlenecks and implementing system-level improvements across people, processes and culture.

Leadership development

Our leadership-development practice is shaped by years of experience with global leadership frameworks such as Dale Carnegie and John Maxwell. With a strong track record in high-impact leadership, team-effectiveness and communication programmes, we design practical, human-centred learning journeys tailored to the evolving needs of leaders at every level.
